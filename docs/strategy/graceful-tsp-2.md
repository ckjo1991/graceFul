# GraceFul – Technical Strategy Proposal
Version 0.2
Author: CK
Status: Early Validation Phase

---

# 1. Problem Statement

Many individuals hesitate to open up about personal struggles in church settings.

Barriers include:
- Fear of judgment
- Social discomfort
- Being new to the community
- Not feeling ready for face to face vulnerability

There is currently no low friction, anonymous entry point for sharing.

---

# 2. Target User

Primary User:
New or hesitant church attendee
- Not fully comfortable yet
- Wants prayer but avoids public sharing
- Needs psychological safety

Secondary User:
Regular member who occasionally prefers anonymous sharing for sensitive topics

GraceFul primarily serves individuals with high sharing friction.

---

# 3. Solution Overview

GraceFul is an anonymous prayer sharing platform where users can:

- Select grateful or struggling
- Choose a life category
- Share anonymously
- Request prayer
- Use optional AI assistance
- Allow categorized viewing
- Request optional private follow up

The platform lowers emotional barriers while preserving the option for deeper connection.

---

# 4. Core Value Proposition

GraceFul provides:

- Psychological safety
- A low pressure first step
- Inclusion for newcomers
- Optional path to real human support

It does not replace in person prayer.
It expands access to it.

---

# 5. Critical Design Requirement

Escalation Option

Users must be able to:
- Indicate if they want someone to reach out
- Feel that support exists beyond the digital platform

This increases trust and prevents isolation.

---

# 6. Benefits

- Encourages participation from silent individuals
- Reduces vulnerability threshold
- Creates structured yet safe sharing environment
- Strengthens perceived community support

---

# 7. Known Risks

- Could be misunderstood as a full replacement for pastoral care
- May require moderation safeguards
- Needs clear boundary messaging

Risk level from this feedback: Low

---

# 8. Success Criteria

GraceFul succeeds if:

- New attendees use it
- Hesitant members begin sharing
- Users escalate to personal connection when ready
- Community perception remains positive

---

# 9. Recommendation

Proceed to lightweight MVP.

Prioritize:
- Anonymous flow clarity
- Optional reach out feature
- Clear positioning language

Continue gathering feedback from additional segments.
